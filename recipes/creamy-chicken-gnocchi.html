Creamy Chicken and Gnocchi

Description

Hearty, cozy, and super easy to whip up. Includes soft-pillowy gnocchi, a garlicy cream sauce,
and tender, juicy chicken thighs.

Ingredients

  • 1 16-ounce package of cauliflower potato gnocchi
  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinnless chicken thighs
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 5 Tbsp unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 medium shallot, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh thyme leaves
  • 3 Tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup half and half
  • 1 bunch kale, stems removed and leaves torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. In a large pot of boiling water, cook gnocchi.
  3. Season chicken with 3/4 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper.
  4. Melt 2 Tbsp butter in a large skillet.
  5. Add chicken to the skillet in a single layer and cook until golden brown.
  6. Melt remaining 3 Tbsp butter. Add shallot and cook until tender.
  7. Whisk flour until lightly browned.
  8. Whisk in chicken broth and win until thickened.
  9. Stir in gnocchi and kale until kale is wilted.
  10. Place into oven and bake until bubbly, about 10-12 minutes.
  11. Serve immediately.
